About the game

Terraria is a 2d survival/sandbox game, the game has a very heavy focus on progression which is acheived through exploring, mining, crafting, and combat to name a few. Terraria features both single and multiplayer options to play with up to 3 of your friends, but that limit can be changed via the very active modding community. Terraria is nearing the end of developement after 13 years and 6 "final" updates, but who knows if thats truely the end.
